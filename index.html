About Me

My name is Julie Acuna, and I am a student at Texas State University. I am majoring in public relations with a minor in psychology. I am interested in communication, media, branding, and the way people connect through storytelling.

My academic interests include public relations writing, visual communication, social media strategy, and human behavior. I like learning how messages can shape the way people understand brands, events, communities, and creative ideas. My psychology minor also helps me think more deeply about audience behavior, identity, and trust.

Outside of school, I enjoy music, fashion, photography, graphic design, and media culture. I like creative projects that allow me to combine writing, visuals, and strategy. This website includes information about my schoolwork, creative interests, experience, and goals.
