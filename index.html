About Me

My name is Julie Acuna, and I am a student at Texas State University majoring in public relations with a minor in psychology. I am interested in communication, media, branding, and the way people connect through storytelling. My academic work has helped me understand how public relations and psychology both connect to audience behavior, identity, and trust.

I enjoy creative projects that allow me to combine writing, design, and strategy. Through my classes, I have worked on assignments involving press releases, campaign ideas, graphic design, visual storytelling, and portfolio-style work. These projects have helped me build skills in both professional communication and creative presentation.

Outside of school, I am interested in music, fashion, psychology, and media culture. I like learning about how people express themselves and how creative industries shape identity. This website includes a little bit about who I am, the work I have created, and the topics that inspire me.
