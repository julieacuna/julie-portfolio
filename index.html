About Me

My name is Julie Acuna, and I am a student at Texas State University studying public relations. I am interested in communication, media, branding, and the way people connect through storytelling. My academic work has helped me understand how messages can influence audiences and create meaningful engagement.

I enjoy creative projects that allow me to combine writing, design, and strategy. Through my classes, I have worked on assignments involving social media planning, press releases, campaign ideas, and portfolio-style work. These projects have helped me build skills in both professional communication and visual presentation.

Outside of school, I am interested in music, fashion, psychology, and media culture. I like learning about how people express themselves and how creative industries shape identity. This website includes a little bit about who I am, the work I have created, and the topics that inspire me.
