My Academic and Creative Work

  • Texas River Keepers Nonprofit Project

    For this public relations project, I created campaign materials for a fictional nonprofit called Texas River Keepers. The project focused on river conservation, community education, and encouraging people to care about local waterways.

    Through this assignment, I learned how to create a message for a specific organization and audience. I also practiced connecting public relations writing with visuals, purpose, and community impact.

  • Press Release Assignment

    I created press releases using a professional format and clear news-style writing. These assignments helped me practice writing headlines, datelines, quotes, and concise paragraphs.

    Press release writing taught me that public relations is not only about promotion. It is also about making information useful, accurate, and easy for journalists or audiences to understand.

  • Graphic Design and Visual Communication

    I have created graphics for public relations campaigns, class assignments, and creative projects. These projects allowed me to think about how color, layout, images, and text can work together to support a message.

    Graphic design helped me understand that visuals are part of communication. A strong design can make information feel more organized, professional, and memorable.

  • Photography and Video Work

    I worked with The University Star as a videographer and photographer. This experience helped me practice visual storytelling in a real student media environment.

    Through photography and video, I learned how to capture moments that tell a story. I also gained experience thinking about composition, timing, and how media can represent campus life.

  • CRM and Client Communication

    In my previous role as a keyholder at Zadig and Voltaire, I focused on CRM and client relationships. I helped build a community of like-minded shoppers through WhatsApp and communicated with clients across the United States and Mexico.

    This experience taught me how relationship-building can support sales, loyalty, and trust. It also connects to public relations because both fields depend on strong communication and understanding an audience.
