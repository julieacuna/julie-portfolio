Experience and Goals

Work and Media Experience

I previously worked as a keyholder at Zadig and Voltaire with a focus on CRM and client relationships. In that role, I helped build rapport with customers and created a community of like-minded shoppers through WhatsApp. This helped generate sales with clients across the United States and Mexico.

My retail experience taught me the importance of personal communication, trust, and consistency. I learned how client relationships can become stronger when people feel remembered, understood, and connected to a brand. This connects closely to public relations because both involve building meaningful relationships with an audience.

I also worked with The University Star as a videographer and photographer. This experience gave me the opportunity to practice visual storytelling and contribute to student media. It helped me become more confident with photography, video, and capturing stories through visuals.

My Goals

My goal is to continue growing as a communicator, writer, and creative professional. I want to keep developing my skills in public relations, media strategy, social media, and visual storytelling.

In the future, I hope to work in a creative communication role where I can combine writing, design, media, and audience strategy. I am interested in work that feels meaningful, intentional, and connected to culture.
